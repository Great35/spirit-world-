About Us

We Are Spirit Word Prophetic Ministry

Welcome to SPIRIT WORD PROPHETIC MINISTRY, a dynamic and Spirit-filled church located in Kubwa, Abuja, Nigeria. Our ministry was pioneered by Prophet Ernest Matt and his wife, Pastor Eva Ernest, under God’s mandate to raise a people of revelation, power, and uncompromising truth.

At SPIRIT WORD PROPHETIC MINISTRY, our mission is clear: to open the eyes of the people to the Word of God with a demonstration of power. To bring the peace of Jesus Christ to the people of the world. We believe that the Gospel is both a message and a manifestation—transforming lives through the light of the Word and the power of the Holy Spirit.

We stand firmly on the pillars of Truth, Love, and Loyalty.
• Truth guides our teaching and our walk with God.
• Love shapes our relationships, outreach, and community life.
• Loyalty defines our commitment to God, to His people, and to the assignment He has given us.

Our services carry a strong prophetic atmosphere, miracles, healings, heartfelt worship, and practical biblical teaching that empowers believers to grow spiritually and walk in their God-given purpose. Whether you are seeking a deeper relationship with God, longing for spiritual direction, or looking for a family of faith, you are welcome here.

Join us at WORD PROPHETIC MINISTRY—where the Word opens your eyes and the Power of God transforms your life.

Our Mission:

To glorify God by spreading the Gospel, empowering our community through service, and nurturing spiritual growth in every individual.

Our Vision:

To be a beacon of hope, love, and faith for all generations, demonstrating Christ’s love through action.

Spirit Word Prophetic Ministry

A place where the Word becomes Spirit and Life. We are committed to raising a generation that walks in the fullness of God's power and purpose.
